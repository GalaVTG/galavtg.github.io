Santa Rosa 17th City Hood Aniversary

July 2021

The Santa Rosa City, Laguna is also known as the Lion City of the South, celebrates its 17th anniversary of the declaration of Santa Rosa Laguna as a city. The Republic Act No 9264 was signed by the former President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo on July 10, 2004, which declare its cityhood. The town started as an agricultural town as most of the citizens were a farmer. The development has boomed with the arrival of industrial factories and real estate developments.


The event was organized by the City Cultural Affairs Office and held indoors inside the Sta. Rosa City Auditorium. The participants have shown case their outstanding talents. The theme of the event is "vaccination for all" - promoting the vaccination of the citizen against the Covid-19 virus. The participants underwent first a COVID-19 antigen test to make sure none of the participants can spread the virus. Another event was a digital art-making contest in which the participants have to submit their digital work online. The topic of the art is "Bakuna ang Pag-asa Ngayong Pandemya". Art can be photo painting, dynamic painting, 2D or 3D graphics, or any digital art graphics.

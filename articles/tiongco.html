The Tiongco Brothers

July 2021

You might be too young to have seen the Tionco Brothers perform, but for Santa Rosa, these artist is what makes the city proud. The Tiongco Brothers (Fernando, Emilio, and Arthuro) are well-known pop artists in the '50s to '70s that live in Santa Rosa. There were so popular that they are dubbed as legendary performers as they gave our country pride and honor. They are the first Filipinos to perform in Sydney Opera in Australia and ARAMCO Saudi Arabia. Their great grandmother is Maria Carpena - the Zarzuela Queen in 1912 - while his father is a well-known pianist. They sang Misa de Gallo during Christmas in the Santa Rosa de Lima Parish Church for more than 54 years.

Student Canteen

The Tiongco Brothers started their career on the Student Canteen TV show in 1958. The show was first aired in ABS-CBN from 1958 to 1965 and then was aired on GMA Network from 1975 to 1986. The show takes the afternoon block (Wikipedia). They start as a competitor in the signing contest of the show until the audience then linking their performance. They become a regular member of the show and then, they started performing in different countries like Japan. One of the members of Tiongco Brothers Emil has once become a city councilor where he serves for many years and later becomes a board member of the Province of Laguna. Jun, one of the members of the Tiongco Brothers pursue a career in business.
